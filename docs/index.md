CardPG is a roleplaying game that sees players exploring dungeons, pushing forward through multiple objectives until their stamina runs out, and they need to return to the safety of town.

## Primary Rolling Mechanic
CardPG uses Fudge Dice. Fudge Dice are D6, and each die has two blank sides, two sides with a +, and two sides with a -. To complete any test, you roll 4DF, and the pluses and minuses cancel out. This leaves you with a numerical result between -4 and +4, with middle results far more likely. This will be added to one of the [Skills](Mechanics/Skills.md), set by the GM.
