Outsiders have found themselves on this plane of existence. They may be a bastard child of a divine or infernal being, or some other otherworldly creature trapped on the mundane plane. They are rare, but generally left alone by those who fear them. In combat, Outsiders use their powers to debilitate their foes.

If you are an outsider, describe the features that expose your otherworldly heritage.

Starting Health: 6
