Constructs are creatures created with cobbled together parts and magic that binds a soul to the new body. Some constructs are made of dead tissue, while others are crafted of wood, metal, and stone. Constructs are rare, but prized as tireless workers. In combat, Constructs empower themselves for extra damage.

If you choose a construct, determine what you were made of, and whose soul now animates you.

Starting Health: 7
