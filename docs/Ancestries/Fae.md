Most Fae in Veleris Coast can be found in the great Fey city of Leriadar, but there are no shortage of Fey creatures who have found their way to the other parts of the land, seeking adventure. Innately magical, Fae have deep ties to the land. In combat, Fae use magic to avoid damage and inflict conditions.

If you choose a Fae, decide what Fey creature you are, and what pushed you into a life of adventure.

Starting Health: 5
