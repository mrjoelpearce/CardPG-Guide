In the Veleris Coast, Dwarves have long abandoned their underground cities in the mountains, and have become a common sight in most settlements. Dwarves are hardy and traditional. In combat, Dwarves excel when taking defensive position against incoming attacks.

If you choose a Dwarf, determine where you hail from and what you carry to tie you to your ancestral home.

Starting Health: 7
