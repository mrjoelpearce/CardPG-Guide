Stealthy and cautious, Halflings are welcome everywhere, running reliable farms and cozy shops. Both of the Halfling towns have fully sunk into the swamps, making most Halflings recent refugees. Large caravans of Halflings have traveled North and West through the mountains, but many have also stayed closer to home. In combat, Halflings are stealthy, but have surprising attacks.

If you choose a Halfling, explain which community you hail from, and describe why you didn't travel with that community beyond the mountains.

Starting Health: 5
