Humanoids with some animal features. Beastkin can be found throughout Veleris Coast, but tend to stay in small communities with their kin. In combat, Beastkin are fierce and mobile fighters.

If you choose to be a Beastkin, describe what animal you resemble.

Starting Health: 6
