Goblins are eager, clever, and endlessly enthusiastic. While they still face some prejudice, especially from Dwarves and Fae, they are excellent hunters, and run many of the most efficient caravan operations in the land. Most towns in the area now feature goblin warrens. In combat, Goblins are remarkably mobile, darting around the battlefield.

If you choose a Goblin, determine where your warren is, and why you are no longer welcome there.

Starting Health: 5
