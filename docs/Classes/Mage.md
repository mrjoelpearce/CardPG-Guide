Cast a range of spells, with the unique ability to use cards from your hand to enhance your attacks. Have a solution to any problem your party faces.

Passive: When you play an Append card standalone (not appended to another attack), deal 1 additional damage.

Starting HP: 4

Recommended Weapons: [Mageblade](../Weapons/Mageblade.md), [Staff](../Weapons/Staff.md), [Tome](../Weapons/Tome.md), [Wand](../Weapons/Wand.md), [Whipblade](../Weapons/Whipblade.md)
