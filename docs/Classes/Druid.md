The Druid uses the power of nature to summon Totems to support zones of allies, and uses nature magic to participate in the fight.

Passive: Your summons have +1 armor.

Starting HP: 6

Recommended Weapons: [Bow](../Weapons/Bow.md), [Cudgel](../Weapons/Cudgel.md), [Mageblade](../Weapons/Mageblade.md), [Staff](../Weapons/Staff.md), [Wand](../Weapons/Wand.md), [Whipblade](../Weapons/Whipblade.md)
