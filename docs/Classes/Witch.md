Curse your enemies, stacking them with negative conditions until they are unable to fight back effectively. Then, let your party take care of them.

Passive: Enemies with 2 or more stacks of Curse deal 1 less damage against you.

Starting HP: 4

Recommended Weapons: [Crossbow](../Weapons/Crossbow.md), [Daggers](../Weapons/Daggers.md), [Mageblade](../Weapons/Mageblade.md), [Staff](../Weapons/Staff.md), [Tome](../Weapons/Tome.md), [Wand](../Weapons/Wand.md)
