Strongest when fighting alone, this melee fighter wants to target individual enemies, then focus them down before switching to new targets. A formidable opponent in a duel.

Passive: Gain Boost when you make an attack with no allies within 3 spaces.

Starting HP: 6

Recommended Weapons: [Brawling](../Weapons/Brawling.md), [Cudgel](../Weapons/Cudgel.md), [Greatsword](../Weapons/Greatsword.md), [Mageblade](../Weapons/Mageblade.md), [Rapier](../Weapons/Rapier.md), [Sword & Shield](../Weapons/Sword%20%26%20Shield.md), [Whipblade](../Weapons/Whipblade.md)
