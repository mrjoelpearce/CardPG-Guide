The Bard is a support caster, providing buffs and debuffs in wide areas, subtly affecting the flow of each battle.

Passive: Whenever you target another ally with an effect, they Cure 1 condition.

Starting HP: 5

Recommended Weapons: [Crossbow](../Weapons/Crossbow.md), [Holy Symbol](../Weapons/Holy%20Symbol.md), [Staff](../Weapons/Staff.md), [Tome](../Weapons/Tome.md), [Wand](../Weapons/Wand.md)
