Make use of stealth with the hide mechanic to weave through the battlefield, choosing targets to strike without remorse. Just stay away from AOE attacks while you do.

Passive: The first time each round you attack from hidden, Restore 1 card from your discard.

Starting HP: 5

Recommended Weapons: [Brawling](../Weapons/Brawling.md), [Crossbow](../Weapons/Crossbow.md), [Cudgel](../Weapons/Cudgel.md), [Daggers](../Weapons/Daggers.md), [Whipblade](../Weapons/Whipblade.md)
