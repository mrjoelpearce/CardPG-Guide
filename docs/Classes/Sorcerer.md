The Sorcerer has the best spells for damage, but few defensive options and many cards with Expend. Make careful choices and use your resources when it counts. The rest of the time, toss around bolts of energy with impunity.

Passive: You may make basic attacks at Range 5.

Starting HP: 8

Recommended Weapons: [Crossbow](../Weapons/Crossbow.md), [Staff](../Weapons/Staff.md), [Sword & Shield](../Weapons/Sword%20%26%20Shield.md), [Tome](../Weapons/Tome.md), [Wand](../Weapons/Wand.md), [Whipblade](../Weapons/Whipblade.md)
