A front-line fighter that benefits from nearby allies. Make use of positioning to burst down particular enemies. Be a presence on the battlefield that can't be ignored.

Passive: If an adjacent enemy moves away, inflict 1 damage.

Starting HP: 7

Recommended Weapons: [Brawling](../Weapons/Brawling.md), [Greataxe](../Weapons/Greataxe.md), [Greatsword](../Weapons/Greatsword.md), [Rapier](../Weapons/Rapier.md), [Sword & Shield](../Weapons/Sword%20%26%20Shield.md), [Warhammer](../Weapons/Warhammer.md)
