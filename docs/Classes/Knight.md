The toughest frontline fighter. Choose a place to make a stand, then refuse to budge.

Passive: Permanent +1 armor.

Starting HP: 8

Recommended Weapons: [Cudgel](../Weapons/Cudgel.md), [Greataxe](../Weapons/Greataxe.md), [Greatsword](../Weapons/Greatsword.md), [Rapier](../Weapons/Rapier.md), [Sword & Shield](../Weapons/Sword%20%26%20Shield.md), [Warhammer](../Weapons/Warhammer.md)
