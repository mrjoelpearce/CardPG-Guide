The Cleric provides healing and buffs to allies, while also having the ability to stand in the midst of the battle when needed.

Passive: Whenever you target an ally with a buff, heal them 1.

Starting HP: 6

Recommended Weapons: [Cudgel](../Weapons/Cudgel.md), [Holy Symbol](../Weapons/Holy%20Symbol.md), [Staff](../Weapons/Staff.md), [Tome](../Weapons/Tome.md), [Warhammer](../Weapons/Warhammer.md)
