The barbarian is a front-line fighter, who uses their card pool as an additional resource. Many of the barbarian's cards require discards for additional effects, but will need to rest more often.

Passive: When you take damage, your next attack this round deals 1 additional damage.

Starting HP: 8

Recommended Weapons: [Greataxe](../Weapons/Greataxe.md), [Greatsword](../Weapons/Greatsword.md), [Cudgel](../Weapons/Cudgel.md), [Warhammer](../Weapons/Warhammer.md), [Brawling](../Weapons/Brawling.md)
