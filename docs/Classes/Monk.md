The monk rushes into battle, and gets stronger when surrounded by enemies. Just be careful not to overextend or get too far from the rest of your party.

Passive: If you hit the same enemy twice in a turn, inflict Weaken 1.

Starting HP: 6

Recommended Weapons: [Brawling](../Weapons/Brawling.md), [Cudgel](../Weapons/Cudgel.md), [Daggers](../Weapons/Daggers.md), [Greatsword](../Weapons/Greatsword.md), [Rapier](../Weapons/Rapier.md), [Sword & Shield](../Weapons/Sword%20%26%20Shield.md), [Whipblade](../Weapons/Whipblade.md)
