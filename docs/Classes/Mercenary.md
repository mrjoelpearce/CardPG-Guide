The Mercenary can fight in the front lines, but also forces enemies to attack each other, using control abilities to manipulate the fight and weaken foes.

Passive: When you attack a target that has a condition applied to them, deal 1 additional damage.

Starting HP: 7

Recommended Weapons: [Brawling](../Weapons/Brawling.md), [Cudgel](../Weapons/Cudgel.md), [Daggers](../Weapons/Daggers.md), [Greatsword](../Weapons/Greatsword.md), [Mageblade](../Weapons/Mageblade.md), [Warhammer](../Weapons/Warhammer.md), [Whipblade](../Weapons/Whipblade.md)
