The Psychic uses mental attacks that bypass enemy defenses, and mind control abilities to coerce enemies to attack each other. Requires thoughtful use of abilities for maximum effect.

Passive: When you kill an enemy, regain 2 health.

Starting HP: 5

Recommended Weapons: [Bow](../Weapons/Bow.md), [Crossbow](../Weapons/Crossbow.md), [Holy Symbol](../Weapons/Holy%20Symbol.md), [Tome](../Weapons/Tome.md), [Wand](../Weapons/Wand.md), [Whipblade](../Weapons/Whipblade.md)
