The Ranger attacks from a distance, and can leave traps in spaces to cause damage and inflict conditions onto anyone stupid enough to walk into (or get pushed into) those spaces. Keep enemies at a distance and pepper them with damage.

Passive: Gain 1 additional range on ranged attacks.

Starting HP: 5

Recommended Weapons: [Bow](../Weapons/Bow.md), [Crossbow](../Weapons/Crossbow.md), [Daggers](../Weapons/Daggers.md), [Wand](../Weapons/Wand.md), [Whipblade](../Weapons/Whipblade.md)
