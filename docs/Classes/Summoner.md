Summoners bind spirits and create an army of minions that move and fight as one. Managing this little army will take some juggling, and losing them can leave the Summoner exposed.

Passive: You can grant your basic moves and attacks to your summoned creatures. 

Starting HP: 4

Recommended Weapons: [Bow](../Weapons/Bow.md), [Crossbow](../Weapons/Crossbow.md), [Holy Symbol](../Weapons/Holy%20Symbol.md), [Tome](../Weapons/Tome.md), [Wand](../Weapons/Wand.md)
