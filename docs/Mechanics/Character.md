Your character is defined by the choices that you make during character creation:

### Ancestry

Your character will be a [Human](../Ancestries/Human.md), [Fae](../Ancestries/Fae.md), [Dwarf](../Ancestries/Dwarf.md), [Goblin](../Ancestries/Goblin.md), [Outsider](../Ancestries/Outsider.md), [Construct](../Ancestries/Construct.md), [Beastkin](../Ancestries/Beastkin.md), or [Halfling](../Ancestries/Halfling.md). Your ancestry will give you 2 cards in combat, and contribute to your starting HP.

### Background

You will also choose how your character grew up. This will give you one card in combat, and will determine your main form of recovery.
* [Noble](../Backgrounds/Noble.md)
* [Farmer](../Backgrounds/Farmer.md)
* [Exile](../Backgrounds/Exile.md)
* [Criminal](../Backgrounds/Criminal.md)
* [Monastic](../Backgrounds/Monastic.md)
* [Military](../Backgrounds/Military.md)
* [Scholar](../Backgrounds/Scholar.md)
* [Wilds](../Backgrounds/Wilds.md)
* [Labourer](../Backgrounds/Labourer.md)
* [Merchant](../Backgrounds/Merchant.md)

### Baggage, Roles and Drives.
Your [Aspects](../Mechanics/Aspects.md) are determined by the combination of choices you've made already.

Your Ancestry and Background determine your **Baggage**, the thing that pushed you into your life of adventure.

Your Ancestry and Class determine your **Role**, the relationship that you have to your party.

Your Background and Class determine your **Drive**, a personal goal that motivates you.
