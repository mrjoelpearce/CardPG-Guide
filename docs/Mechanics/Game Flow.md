The overall flow of the game is as follows:

The group will be operating out of a settlement. NPCs at that settlement will offer tasks that can be taken on by the group.  Every settlement has a few [Dungeons](../Mechanics/Dungeons.md) nearby, which offer adventuring opportunities. At the settlement, the party will use [Adventuring](../Mechanics/Adventuring.md) skills to learn more information.

Then, the group will set out to a Dungeon.  Before they set out, they need to:

* Determine a hand of cards from their [Card Pool](../Mechanics/Card%20Pool.md)
* Set up their [Equipment](../Mechanics/Equipment.md)

Once in a dungeon, the group will traverse zones of the dungeon. Some of these will feature [Adventuring](../Mechanics/Adventuring.md) opportunities, and others will be [Combat](../Mechanics/Combat.md) encounters.

As players work through these encounters, they will resolve objectives and earn gold by fighting enemies. At the end of each zone, the party will decide whether to continue or turn back, depending on how many of their cards are running out.

If the party leaves a dungeon, it will probably change and be repopulated with new dangers if they want to return to push deeper into the dungeon another time.

Once the players have cleared the dungeons in an area, they will move on to another settlement, where more adventures await.
