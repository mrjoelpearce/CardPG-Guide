Your pool of cards will be determined by your character creation choices. 

You will use your card pool to determine a hand of 12 cards for your adventure.

| Level | Cards unlocked | Source         | Notes                                                           |

| 1     | 7              | **weapon**     | Core weapon identity — players choose **5** of 7 for their pool |
| 1     | 2              | **ancestry**   | Abilities granted by your ancestry                              |
| 1     | 1              | **background** | A single card that focuses on recovery                          |
| 2     | 2              | **class**      | Added class cards for further customization                     |
| 3     | 2              | **weapon**     | Advanced weapon moves - get both                                |
| 4     | 1              | **subclass**   | Subclass chosen here: hand size increases to 13                 |

| 8     | 1              | **subclass**   | Final subclass card; completes the set. Hand size: 15           |
