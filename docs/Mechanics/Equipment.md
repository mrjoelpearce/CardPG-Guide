You have five equipment slots, that work much like cards.

**Armor**: Your armor may grant you passive armor, as well as having active abilities that prevent damage.

**Headgear**: Helmets can protect you from critical hits, but can also have a number of other practical uses.

**Trinket**: Trinkets have minor beneficial effects that can get you out of a tight spot

**Potions**: Potions are used for restoration, healing or restoring cards to keep you in the fight.

**Trophy**: Major enemies drop Trophies, powerful single-use items that can turn the tide of a fight.
