### Passive Abilities
Every class and every weapon grants a **passive ability** that is always active — no card needs to be played to trigger it. These can significantly shape how you approach fights.

#### Classes

- [Barbarian](../Classes/Barbarian.md): When you take damage, your next attack this round deals 1 additional damage.
- [Bard](../Classes/Bard.md): Whenever you target another ally with an effect, they Cure 1 condition.
- [Cleric](../Classes/Cleric.md): Whenever you target an ally with a buff, heal them 1.
- [Druid](../Classes/Druid.md): Your summons have +1 armor.
- [Knight](../Classes/Knight.md): Permanent +1 armor.
- [Mage](../Classes/Mage.md): When you play an Append card standalone (not appended to another attack), deal 1 additional damage.
- [Mercenary](../Classes/Mercenary.md): When you attack a target that has a condition applied to them, deal 1 additional damage.
- [Monk](../Classes/Monk.md): If you hit the same enemy twice in a turn, inflict Weaken 1.
- [Psychic](../Classes/Psychic.md): When you kill an enemy, regain 2 health.
- [Ranger](../Classes/Ranger.md): Gain 1 additional range on ranged attacks.
- [Rogue](../Classes/Rogue.md): The first time each round you attack from hidden, Restore 1 card from your discard.
- [Soldier](../Classes/Soldier.md): If an adjacent enemy moves away, inflict 1 damage.
- [Sorcerer](../Classes/Sorcerer.md): You may make basic attacks at Range 5.
- [Summoner](../Classes/Summoner.md): You can grant your basic moves and attacks to your summoned creatures.
- [Swashbuckler](../Classes/Swashbuckler.md): Gain Boost when you make an attack with no allies within 3 spaces.
- [Witch](../Classes/Witch.md): Enemies with 2 or more stacks of Curse deal 1 less damage against you.

#### Weapons

- [Bow](../Weapons/Bow.md): The first time each round an enemy enters an adjacent space, you may move 1.
- [Brawling](../Weapons/Brawling.md): Your second and subsequent attacks each turn deal 1 additional damage.
- [Crossbow](../Weapons/Crossbow.md): If a single-target attack does 3+ damage, it also hits the enemy directly behind the target.
- [Cudgel](../Weapons/Cudgel.md): When you deal 3 or more damage in a single attack, apply Stun.
- [Daggers](../Weapons/Daggers.md): Your attacks that deal Bleed, Weaken, or Poison deal one additional stack.
- [Greataxe](../Weapons/Greataxe.md): If you deal 4 damage to an enemy in a single hit, inflict Weaken 1.
- [Greatsword](../Weapons/Greatsword.md): When you attack, deal one extra damage if you've moved this turn.
- [Holy Symbol](../Weapons/Holy%20Symbol.md): At the start of each round of combat, gain Bless.
- [Mageblade](../Weapons/Mageblade.md): The first time each round you apply an elemental condition (Burn, Freeze, or Shock) with an attack, restore 1 card from your discard.
- [Rapier](../Weapons/Rapier.md): When an enemy's melee attack deals no damage, immediately attack 1 against them.
- [Staff](../Weapons/Staff.md): Allies are immune to your AoE attacks.
- [Sword & Shield](../Weapons/Sword%20%26%20Shield.md): The first time you attack each round, gain Bolster 1.
- [Tome](../Weapons/Tome.md): Whenever you apply Curse to an enemy, they also take 1 damage.
- [Wand](../Weapons/Wand.md): Your first attack each round gains Bypass.
- [Warhammer](../Weapons/Warhammer.md): Your melee attacks add Pierce 1.
- [Whipblade](../Weapons/Whipblade.md): When you Retaliate, you may move one space.
