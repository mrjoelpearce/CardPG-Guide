    Character

    Your character is defined by the choices that you make during character creation:

    Ancestry¶

    Your character will be a Human, Fae, Dwarf, Goblin, Outsider, Construct, Beastkin, or Halfling. Your ancestry will give you 2 cards in combat, and contribute to your starting HP.

    Background¶

    You will also choose how your character grew up. This will give you one card in combat, and will determine your main form of recovery.

    Subclass¶

    At Level 4, you will choose a Subclass, which will either reinforce your class choice, or subvert it slightly.

    Subclass Parent Role Description
    Hag Witch Reinforce More Curses, tricky moves
    Necromancer Witch Subvert Raise undead minions from fallen foes as ongoing cards
    Godtouched Cleric Reinforce Healing and targeted support
    Retributor Cleric Subvert Non-elemental damage spells and card recovery
    Battlemage Mage Reinforce Maintains spells but adds mobility and protection, with spells to append onto
    Pyromancer Mage Subvert Rain down fire on everyone
    Shapeshifter Druid Subvert Ongoing and single-use cards that let the druid make physical attacks near totems
    Elementalist Druid Reinforce Strong elemental magic and zones of elemental power
    Trapper Ranger Subvert A mix of ranged attacks and control, with ongoing animal summons
    Sharpshooter Ranger Reinforce Borrows the Mage's append mechanic to add effects to attacks
    Bounty Hunter Mercenary Reinforce More control options and additional coerce cards
    Mage Hunter Mercenary Subvert Attacks specializing in bypass and condition clearing, with mobility to reach the back line
    Paladin Knight Subvert Adds some magical healing and further tanking
    Templar Knight Reinforce Continues with retaliation, but more offense than defense this time
    Captain Soldier Reinforce More ongoing cards, and cards that call allies to action
    Gladiator Soldier Subvert Fighting dirty, with more push and pull effects
    Martial Artist Monk Reinforce More direct combat moves
    Ascendant Monk Subvert Adds recovery and healing, turning enemy attacks back on them
    Duellist Swashbuckler Reinforce Additional effects that focus on a single target enemy
    Brawler Swashbuckler Subvert Still a mobile fighter, but now uses show-off fighting moves against groups
    Burglar Rogue Subvert Leans into stealth and stealing to regain cards
    Assassin Rogue Reinforce Leans into cards that deal extra damage from stealth, poison, and bleed
    Skald Bard Reinforce More storytelling, with ongoing cards that buff allies and hinder enemies
    Trickster Bard Subvert Illusion magic and tricks that mess with the battlefield itself
    Champion Barbarian Subvert Target and burst down specific enemies, with rewards for killing foes
    Berserker Barbarian Reinforce More rage, more card cycling, more mayhem
    Manipulator Psychic Reinforce More direct control on your enemies
    Souleater Psychic Subvert Gain power from the damage you deal
    Blood Mage Sorcerer Reinforce Fuel powerful spells with your own life
    Warlock Sorcerer Subvert Turn your basic attacks into powerful blasts
    Demonologist Summoner Subvert Summon powerful creatures you might be able to control
    Primal Summoner Reinforce Summon elementals to aid you

    Baggage, Roles and Drives.¶

    Your Aspects are determined by the combination of choices you've made already.

    Your Ancestry and Background determine your Baggage, the thing that pushed you into your life of adventure.

    Your Ancestry and Class determine your Role, the relationship that you have to your party.

    Your Background and Class determine your Drive, a personal goal that motivates you.
