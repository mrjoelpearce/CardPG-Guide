    Home

    CardPG is a roleplaying game that sees players exploring dungeons, pushing forward through multiple objectives until their stamina runs out, and they need to return to the safety of town.

    Primary Rolling Mechanic¶

    CardPG uses Fudge Dice. Fudge Dice are D6, and each die has two blank sides, two sides with a +, and two sides with a -. To complete any test, you roll 4DF, and the pluses and minuses cancel out. This leaves you with a numerical result between -4 and +4, with middle results far more likely. This will be added to one of the Skills, set by the GM.

    Characters¶

    Your Character will be created by making a series of choices.

    Ancestry: Beastkin, Construct, Dwarf, Fae, Goblin, Halfling, Human, Outsider

    Background: Criminal, Exile, Farmer, Labourer, Merchant, Military, Monastic, Noble, Scholar, Wilds

    Class: Barbarian, Bard, Cleric, Druid, Knight, Mage, Mercenary, Monk, Psychic, Ranger, Rogue, Soldier, Sorcerer, Summoner, Swashbuckler, Witch

    Weapon: Bow, Brawling, Crossbow, Cudgel, Daggers, Greataxe, Greatsword, Holy Symbol, Mageblade, Rapier, Staff, Sword & Shield, Tome, Wand, Warhammer, Whipblade
